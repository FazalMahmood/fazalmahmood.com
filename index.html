Nextcloud

Online File Storage

Nextcloud is a suite of client-server software that enables the creation and utilization of file hosting services. It offers functionality similar to popular services such as Dropbox, or Google Drive, particularly when used alongside integrated office suites with Collabora Online.

AppFlowy

Project Management

AppFlowy offers an open-source option as an alternative to Notion, providing control over your data and customization options.

WordPress

Websites & Forums

Originally developed as a blogging tool, WordPress has evolved into a comprehensive web content management system supporting various types of content, such as traditional websites, mailing lists, and Internet forums.

Jitsi Meet

Video Conferencing

A VoIP and instant messaging application that includes video conferencing features.

Cal

Online Meeting Scheduling

An open-source meeting scheduling infrastructure tailored for personal or business needs.

Baserow

Web Forms

Baserow is an open-source, user-friendly online database tool that simplifies database creation and management without programming. It's versatile, scalable, and suitable for personal and enterprise projects.

Invoice Ninja

Online Invoicing Software

An online invoicing platform, available for free, assists in generating and managing invoices, accepting payments, tracking time, and syncing with your bank and inventory. It allows you to customize invoices and integrate with payment gateways.

Dolibarr

Business Collaboration

An open-source ERP and CRM software designed for small to medium-sized enterprises, offering features like invoicing, accounting, inventory, and project management for efficient business management.

Jellyfin

Media Server

A media system that enables streaming of personal media to any device from a self-hosted server. Jellyfin allows users to enjoy movies, shows, music, live TV, books, and photos, all while maintaining complete control over their data.
